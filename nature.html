NATURAL PRODUCTS

The term “natural” is often misconstrued as solely consisting of ingredients derived from nature. But even to this day, there isn’t a universally accepted or regulated definition of what constitutes a “natural” skincare product. In reality, the label “natural” is often employed to denote the absence of synthetic and artificial additives such as preservatives, fragrances, and colorants, rather than the presence of naturally-sourced ingredients.
