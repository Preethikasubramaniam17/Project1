ORGANIC PRODUCTS

In skincare, the term “organic” describes products made with organically-farmed ingredients that are grown without synthetic pesticides, fertilizers, or other harmful chemicals. Unfortunately, the situation is quite murky when it comes to labeling beauty products as “organic”. That is because the FDA does not oversee or enforce regulations for the use of this term. Yes, that means it’s up to us, the consumers, to confirm its authenticity.
